About Bill

Bill Gabriel is a photographer based in the western suburbs of Chicago, specializing in sports, event, and portrait photography. With an eye for decisive moments and natural light, he brings energy and authenticity to every shoot.

Whether it's freezing the peak action of a basketball game, documenting the memory of a family celebration, or creating a timeless portrait, Bill's goal is always the same — capture the joy of the moment.

When he's not behind the lens, you can find him coaching youth sports, exploring the outdoors, or testing out new camera gear he definitely didn't need.
